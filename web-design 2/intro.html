I'm Caocao

  • I'm an ordinary girl,
  • having a secret story,
  • dreaming my own dream.

Unlike all the other girls,

Shopping, cooking, gossiping... are not my type.

I tried to get involved into their circle,

But I really don't know how to make friends.

I'm afraid of the crowds, loud sounds, celebration kind of things.

I love being alone,

Shut the shutters,

chatting with myself.

Sometimes,

I play with my little ukulele,

Singing to myself.

I have only several friends, can counted by a hand.

They know me well, reading me like a book.

We usually sing together, but more like whispering

They know me,

I love quiet.
